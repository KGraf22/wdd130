Overview

Purpose

Bring adventure to people with quality equipment and have experienced and qualified guides to keep them safe.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life situation. Middle aged person who works an office job with no excitement in their life. They are tired of the same old routine and want to change it up. What do they want? Excitement and adventure with little legistics. What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will pick how long their ride will be (full length, partial or a specialty), a calendar to reserve a guide. They will be able to pay for their reservation. They will be able to get contact information with us if they have any questions.

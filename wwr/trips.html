Trip Descriptions

Trip 1: River Expedition

This package includes a 3 mile trip down the Snake River with little turbulent waters best suited for those just trying to relax and for beginners. Lunches will be provided.

Trip 2: White Water Thrill

This package includes 10 miles of fun filled thrill. Lunches will be provided.

Trip 3: Extreme Adventure

This package includes a 20 mile ride stopping at mile 10 for an over night glamping at our Rafting lodge. Breakfast, lunch and dinner will be provided.
